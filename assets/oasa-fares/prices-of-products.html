Ticket Fares
Tickets and regular ticket packs may be loaded in all means of transport where Athenaticket and Athenacard are used (anonymous and personalized).

Discount tickets and the respective ticket packs may be loaded only on personalized Athenacards.

SINGLE TICKETS

90-MINUTE SINGLE TICKET

Full price: 1,20 €
Discounted: 0,50 €

Exclude Airport & X80
The single ticket is valid for all Athens Urban Transport Organization means: buses, trolley-buses, trams and metro lines 1, 2 and 3 (to Koropi Station) networks and for the Suburban Railway (TRAINOSE) (route part including Magoula – Piraeus – Koropi).
This ticket type is not valid for Airport EXPRESS bus lines, that is X80 bus line, and metro line 3 for the route part Koropi – Airport.
Route to Airport & X80 bus line not included
The single ticket’s validity term is 90 minutes since its first validation.

Valid for 24 hours from its first validation.
Not valid for EXPRESS bus lines to Airport and metro line 3 for the route part Koropi – Airport.

Full price: 8,20 €

Valid for 5 X 24 hours from its first validation.

3-DAY TOURIST TICKET

Full price: 20,00 €
Discounted: –

2-way from/to Airport
The 3-day tourist ticket is valid for 3 X 24 hours from its first validation for all Athens Urban Transport Organization (OASA) means: buses, trolley-buses, trams and metro lines 1, 2 and 3 (to Koropi Station) networks and for the Suburban Railway (TRAINOSE) (route part including Magoula – Piraeus – Koropi). It also includes a 2-way travel from and to the Athens International Airport by the Metro or the respective EXPRESS bus lines.
This ticket is also valid for X80 bus line.

The single ticket is also available at discount packs of 2, 5 and 10+1 tickets. Discount tickets and the respective package offers are loaded only on personalized Athenacards.

When you buy a 5 or 10+1-ticket pack at a total price of €6,50 or € €13,50 respectively, your are charged with the maximum fare per day. This means that if you validate 4 tickets out of your ticket pack in one day, you won’t be charged with any other fare for the rest of your travels within that same day. (Keep on validating your card/ticket, without having to validate another ticket).

Stored Value

Stored value operates as a wallet you may use for your transportation instead of buying specific tickets in ATH.ENA CARD cards (anonymous and personalized). It may added supplementary to any product we have loaded them. The maximum amount of stored value that can be loaded is 50 euros. 

It is appropriate whenever our card has a remaining number of tickets for urban transportation and we want to go to the Airport or use the X80 bus line (e.g. when our card has unlimited number of journeys for urban transportation or a number of urban zone tickets, we may add the respective price €6 or €10 depending on the means of transport we will choose for our transfer to the Airport). 

Note! The stored value of €30 is not equivalent to nor may be converted to a 30-day product despite the fact that it has the same value. 

  • The ATH.ENA CARD holders may reload the card with a second product prior to the expiry of the first one.The new product will be activated upon expiry of the current one, by the first validation of the card. 
  • The maximum charge per day for transportation within the urban zone is equivalent to 4 single tickets, in the event of purchase of a package of tickets, and the price of a daily ticket amounting to 4.10 euros, in the event of use of stored value.
